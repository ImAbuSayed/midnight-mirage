# Raw Content: Midnight Mirage


<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-song">Song</h2>



<p>Midnight Mirage</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-lyrics">Lyrics</h2>



<p><em>Your shadow dances close in the neon’s lazy glow,</em><br><em>Two strangers in a crowd, but your eyes scream, “I know.”</em><br><em>You spin a lie, say you’re leavin’, but your heels move slow,</em><br><em>Lipstick stains on your glass, and the DJ drops it low…</em></p>



<p><em>Say you’re done, but your fingers trace the edge of my sleeve,</em><br><em>Whisper, “Not tonight,” then you bite your lip at me.</em><br><em>We’re a paradox, yeah, a fire dressed in freeze—</em><br><em>If this is wrong, baby, wrong’s where I wanna be.</em></p>



<p><em>Ride the rhythm, ride the silence, ride this ache,</em><br><em>(Ooh, let it break)</em><br><em>Pull me closer just to push me all away.</em><br><em>Ride the line between the truth and our mistakes,</em><br><em>Midnight mirage—I’m still chasin’ what you make…</em></p>



<p><em>Your phone buzzes loud, but you crush it with a stare,</em><br><em>Draggin’ me to the floor like the world’s burnin’ there.</em><br><em>Laughin’ at my jokes, but your nails dig my arm bare,</em><br><em>We’re a storm in a glass, darlin’, tip it if you dare.</em></p>



<p><em>Now the taxi’s here, but you’re frozen in the door,</em><br><em>Say you’ll stay, then you flinch at the word “more.”</em><br><em>Is this love or just the wine? I don’t care anymore—</em><br><em>Paint me guilty, let the city be our courtroom floor.</em></p>



<p><em>Midnight mirage, fade to nothin’ with the dawn,</em><br><em>But your ghost’s in my sheets, hummin’ our reckless song.</em><br><em>Ride it… ride it… till the memories are gone…</em><br><em>Fade to breathy vocals and a lone synth’s mournful thrum.</em></p>
